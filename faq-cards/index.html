Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you need an FAQ section?

FAQs are an integral part of the user experience and their objective is to preempt as many questions as possible from the user.

Is an FAQ section the same as a knowledge base?

FAQ sections usually consist of a series of categories and questions with concise answers across one or a few pages. A knowledge base is usually an extensive directory with comprehensive articles accessed by a search facility.

Where to find questions for FAQ?

Customer services, Site search, Google search console, Quora and other questions sites, Keyword research...

How to find questions for FAQs with a keyword research tool?

The Keyword Magic tool can help you to find questions to answer for your FAQs. There are two ways to find questions: by searching around keywords and by searching branded keywords.

What is FAQ Schema?

Circa May 2019, Google introduced FAQ and How-to structured data as a variety of rich results. The schema allowed a series of accordion questions to appear below a SERP listing.
